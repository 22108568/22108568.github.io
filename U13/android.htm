Android Phones

Android phones are known to be cheap and reliable devices, but also heavily modifiable. Newer and more powerful android phones have the capability to be heavily customised to your liking. Unlike the limitations of Apple and Samsung devices, other android phones can be changed into doing what you would desire it to do, meaning that you can completely rebuild the phone and it's operating system if you wanted to. This is how hackers are even less detectible as they don't just carry a laptop with themselves to do their job. All they need is an android phone.
